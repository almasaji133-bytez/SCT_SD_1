## 🔢 Conversion Formulas

| From | To Fahrenheit | To Kelvin |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| Celsius (°C) | °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32 | K = °C + 273.15 |
| Fahrenheit (°F) | — | K = (°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273.15 |
| Kelvin (K) | °F = (K − 273.15) × 9/5 + 32 | — |

| From | To Celsius |
|------|-----------|
| Fahrenheit (°F) | °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9 |
| Kelvin (K) | °C = K − 273.15 |
